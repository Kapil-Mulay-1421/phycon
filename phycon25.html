Highlights of the Conference

PHYCON-25, held at IIT Ropar on 21–22 March 2025, marked a significant milestone in the institute’s scientific journey, bringing together over 250+ researchers, academicians, and students from across India.

The conference was graced by eminent speakers — Prof. S. B. Ogale, Prof. Anand Kumar Jha, Prof. M. Krishnamurthy, Prof. Peter Kruse, and Prof. Raghunath Sahoo — who delivered inspiring plenary talks across diverse frontiers of physics.

The event was inaugurated under the leadership of Prof. Rajeev Ahuja, Director of IIT Ropar, whose vision for interdisciplinary research and innovation set the tone for the conference.

With vibrant student presentations, active industry participation, and rich scientific dialogue, PHYCON-25 stood out as a major achievement in fostering collaboration, advancing cutting-edge research, and strengthening the bridge between academia and real-world applications.
